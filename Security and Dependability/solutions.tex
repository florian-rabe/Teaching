\begin{exsolution}{exc:sd:railfence}
Let $f=l/k$ be the length of the fence.
Then $\tau_k(i) = (i\modop k)\cdot f + (i\divop k)$.
Its inverse is $\tau_k^{-1}(j)=(j\modop f)\cdot k + (j\divop f)$.
\end{exsolution}
